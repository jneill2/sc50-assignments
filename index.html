Basic Class Packages

Here are three common packages that students start with. You can sign up for as much or as little as you would like to start out with, then book more as you decide to dig deeper into Biblical Greek. All classes are at an affordable tuition.

1st Immersion of the BGP

Start by signing up for this intense 10-day immersion course. Learn to interact with the Greek text through role-plays, storytelling, and much more. This is the first level (Level 1A) of the Biblical Greek Program, so you will be all set to continue studying online afterwards.

Book This Class
1 Year of the BGP

Enjoy the stories and role-plays in year 1 of the Biblical Greek Program. You will start with a 10-day immersion, which is followed by a 15-week class of online video conferencing. The year is completed with another action-packed 10-day immersion. You did it!

Book This Class
2 Years of the BGP

Sign up for two years of Biblical Greek immersion. Grammar comes to life as you immerse yourself in stories from Acts and the epistle to the Galatians. The two years finish with another immersion class full of role-plays, interactive grammar drills, and speeches. Congratulations!

Book This Class

Tuition

Sign up for as much or as little of the Biblical Greek Program as you want to participate in. However, every Level has the previous Levels as prerequisites.

When you sign up for Year 1, you are signing up for two immersion courses and one online course. Those two immersion courses and that one online course are roughly equivalent to two college semesters. These three components constitute one full year of Greek. Year 2 is similar to Year 1. By signing up for Year 2 you are signing up for two online courses and one immersion course at the end.
